# Route 4 options

## Short Tweespruit Route
```csv
Destination,Route,Distance
Excelsior,R709,35
Verkeerdevlei,R703,30
Bloemfontein,R703 to R700 to N1,73
```
from Tweespruit you can take a shortened route to Excelsior, Verkeerdevlei and back
There are also some other small towns on this route you can adjust for.

## To Winburg

from Winburg there are 3 other routes to take:
1. Directly back to Bloemfontein (via N1)
2. Skip toll gate to Verkeerdevlei (N1 and R703)
3. The long way around Theunissen and Winnie Mandela (R708, R30 ,N1)

### Direct Route
```csv
Destination,Route,Distance
Bloemfontein,N1,112
```
This just goes straight back to Bloem and has no extra stops
You basically just skip Verkeerdevlei

### Verkeerdevlei Route
```csv
Destination,Route,Distance
Verkeerdevlei,N1 to R703,55
Bloemfontein,R703 to R700 to N1,73
```
here you just "go around" the tool gate

### The long way
```csv
Destination,Route,Distance
Theunissen,R708,36
Winnie Mandela,R30,44
Bloemfontein,R30 to N1,62
```
This is a bit longer but has 2 extra stops
